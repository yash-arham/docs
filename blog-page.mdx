The **Default Blog** template in the Sentiment theme is built from four stacked sections — a Blog header masthead, a Featured article spotlight, a Blog posts grid, and a Newsletter signup. Each section can be configured independently from the Theme Editor to control the full look and feel of your journal listing page.

  ![Blog Listing Page](/images/blog-page.webp)
</Frame>

### How to Configure the Blog Page

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes** and click **Customize**.
2. From the top page dropdown, select **Blogs → Journal** (or your blog name).
3. Click any section in the left sidebar under **Template** to select and configure it.
4. Click **Save** when done.

The blog listing page template contains these sections:

- **Blog header** — editorial masthead with heading and breadcrumb
- **Featured article** — spotlight card for the most recent or pinned post
- **Blog posts grid** — paginated grid of all articles
- **Newsletter** — email signup at the bottom of the listing page

---

### Blog Header Section Settings

The Blog Header section is the masthead at the top of the listing page. It includes a breadcrumb, eyebrow, heading, and right-column blocks for text, stats, and a CTA button.

<Frame>
  ![Blog Header Settings](/images/blog-settings-header.webp)
</Frame>

#### Breadcrumb

- **Show breadcrumb:** Toggle to show or hide the breadcrumb navigation bar above the heading.
- **Parent label:** The label inserted between "Index" and the blog title in the breadcrumb path (e.g., "Editorial"). Leave blank to omit the parent step.
- **Breadcrumb meta text:** Small text shown on the right side of the breadcrumb bar. Use bold for emphasis words (e.g., "New writing every **Thursday**").

#### Header Content

- **Eyebrow label:** A small label displayed above the main heading (e.g., "Editorial · The Journal").
- **Heading:** The main blog masthead heading. Supports rich text and italic emphasis (e.g., "Notes from _the bench._").

#### Style

- **Color scheme:** Choose a color scheme to control the section's background and text colors.

#### Spacing

- **Padding top / Padding bottom:** Control the vertical spacing around the Blog header section.

### Featured Article Section Settings

The Featured Article section displays a large spotlight card for one article — typically your most recent or most important post. It sits directly below the Blog header and above the posts grid.

For full configuration details, see the [Featured article](featured-article) section documentation.

Key settings:

- **Featured article:** Pin a specific article, or leave blank to auto-show the most recent post.
- **Show featured badge / label:** Toggle and customize the "Featured" badge on the card.
- **Show read time / Show author:** Toggle metadata shown on the card.
- **Image ratio / Image fit:** Control how the article image is displayed.
- **Color scheme / Padding:** Style and spacing controls.

### Blog Posts Grid Section Settings

The Blog Posts Grid section renders the full paginated grid of articles from your blog below the featured article spotlight.

#### Content

- **Eyebrow:** Small label above the heading.
- **Heading:** Section heading displayed above the grid.
- **Heading typography:** Select the typographic style for the heading.
- **View all label:** Text for the "view all" link at the bottom of the grid.

#### Source

- **Blog:** Select the blog to pull articles from.
- **Articles to show:** Number of articles to display per page before pagination (range: 2–12).

#### Display

- **Full width:** When enabled, the grid stretches edge-to-edge.
- **Columns desktop:** Number of columns on desktop (2, 3, or 4).
- **Image ratio:** Aspect ratio for article card images — Portrait, Tall portrait, Square, Landscape, or Adapt.
- **Image fit:** Controls how images fill their card — Cover or Contain.
- **Show author:** Toggle to display the author's name on each article card.
- **Show date:** Toggle to display the publish date on each card.
- **Show read time:** Toggle to display the estimated read time on each card.
- **Show tags:** Toggle to display the article's tags as chips on each card.

#### Style

- **Color scheme:** Choose a color scheme for the grid section.

#### Spacing

- **Padding top / Padding bottom:** Control the vertical spacing around the grid.

---

### Newsletter Section Settings

The Newsletter section appears at the bottom of the blog listing page as a full-width email signup area.

- **Heading:** Heading above the signup form.
- **Subtext:** Supporting description text below the heading.
- **Email placeholder:** Placeholder text inside the email input field.
- **Submit text:** Label on the submit button.
- **Success text:** Message shown after a successful subscription.
- **Color scheme:** Choose a color scheme for the newsletter section.
- **Padding top / Padding bottom:** Control vertical spacing.
