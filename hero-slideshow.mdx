---
title: "Hero slideshow"
---

The **Hero Slideshow** section is a full-screen auto-advancing slideshow with up to 6 slides. Each slide has its own background image, heading, body text, dual CTAs, and a navigation label. It's ideal for homepages where multiple campaigns, collections, or seasons need to be featured simultaneously.

### How to Add the Section

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes** and click **Customize**.
2. Navigate to your homepage.
3. Click **Add section** and select **Hero slideshow**.
4. Add **Slide** blocks and configure each one individually.
5. Click **Save**.

### Section Settings

#### Persistent Labels

- **Top left:** A text label displayed in the top-left corner across all slides simultaneously.
- **Top right:** A text label displayed in the top-right corner across all slides simultaneously.

#### Behaviour

- **Interval:** The time in milliseconds before the slideshow automatically advances to the next slide (default: 6000ms / 6 seconds).

#### Style

- **Color scheme:** Choose a color scheme for the slideshow navigation UI.

---

### Slide Block Settings

Each individual slide added within the Hero slideshow has its own settings panel. You can add up to 6 slides.

#### Image

- **Image:** Upload the background image for this slide.

#### Content

- **Eyebrow:** A small label displayed above the heading.
- **Heading:** Main heading for the slide.
- **Heading italic:** An italic portion of the heading, rendered in the italic type style alongside the main heading.
- **Body:** Supporting body text for the slide.
- **Meta:** A pipe-separated metadata string displayed below the body (e.g., `18k Au | 24 pieces | Winter 26`).

#### Call to Action

- **CTA 1 text / URL:** Primary call-to-action button label and link.
- **CTA 2 text / URL:** Secondary call-to-action button label and link.

#### Navigation

- **Nav label:** The label shown in the slide navigation dots at the bottom of the slideshow for this specific slide.
