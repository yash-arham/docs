title: "Predictive search"
---

The **Predictive Search** is a layout-level component that appears when customers type in the search field in the header. It displays live product, article, and page results as the customer types, without requiring them to submit a full search query.

Predictive search is a built-in theme component powered by the Shopify Search & Discovery API — it is not a standalone section and does not have its own settings panel in the theme editor.

### How to Improve Predictive Search Results

To improve the quality of search results and ensure the right products surface, use Shopify's **Search & Discovery** app:

- From your Shopify admin, go to **Apps → Search & Discovery**.
- Use **Synonyms** to map common customer search terms to your product vocabulary.
- Use **Boosts** to pin high-priority products to the top of results for specific queries.
- Use **Filters** to configure which product attributes are available in the collection filter.
