The **Centered CTA** section is a focused call-to-action block with a centered eyebrow, heading, supporting text, and two buttons (primary and secondary). It's designed to drive a specific action at the end of a page, between content sections, or as a standalone campaign prompt — such as booking a consultation, exploring a collection, or signing up.

### How to Add the Section

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes** and click **Customize**.
2. Navigate to the page where you want to add this section.
3. Click **Add section** and select **Centered CTA**.
4. Fill in the heading, text, and button settings.
5. Click **Save**.

### Section Settings

#### Content

- **Eyebrow:** Small label displayed above the heading.
- **Heading:** Main heading for the CTA. Supports rich text and italic emphasis.
- **Subtext:** Supporting description text below the heading.
- **Primary label / URL:** Text and link destination for the primary (filled) button.
- **Secondary label / URL:** Text and link destination for the secondary (ghost/outline) button.

#### Style

- **Full width:** When enabled, the section stretches edge-to-edge.
- **Color scheme:** Choose a color scheme for the section.

#### Spacing

- **Padding top / Padding bottom:** Control vertical spacing around the section.
