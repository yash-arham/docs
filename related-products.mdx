---

The **Related Products** section renders a full-width product grid below the main product content, showing items from the same collection under a configurable heading. It keeps customers engaged after viewing a product by surfacing contextually relevant pieces from your catalog.

<Frame>
  ![Related Products](/images/product-page-17.webp)
</Frame>

### How to Add the Section

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes** and click **Customize**.
2. Navigate to a product page.
3. Click **Add section** and select **Related products**.
4. Configure the settings below and click **Save**.

---

### Section Settings

#### Content

- **Eyebrow:** Small label above the heading (e.g., "Continue · The family").
- **Heading:** Main section heading (e.g., "You may also like.").
- **Typography:** Typographic preset for the heading (e.g., Heading 2).
- **View all text:** Label for the "View all" link (default: "View all").
- **View all URL:** Destination for the "View all" link. Leave blank to auto-link to the source collection.

#### Source

- **Source collection:** Select a specific collection to pull related products from. If left blank, the section automatically uses the current product's first collection.
- **Number of products:** Number of product cards to show in the grid (range: 2–8, default: 4).
