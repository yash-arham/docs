---
title: "Product carousel"
---

The **Product Carousel** section displays a horizontally scrollable row of product cards from a selected collection, paired with an editorial heading, collection metadata fields, and a view-all link. It gives customers a preview of a collection without committing them to a full grid — ideal for homepages, campaign pages, and "you may also like" placements.

### How to Add the Section

1. From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes** and click **Customize**.
2. Navigate to the page where you want to add this section.
3. Click **Add section** and select **Product carousel**.
4. Select a collection, fill in the content fields, and click **Save**.

### Section Settings

#### Content

- **Eyebrow:** Small label above the heading.
- **Heading:** Main section heading.
- **Meta 1 / 2 / 3 — Label & Value:** Three metadata pairs displayed alongside the heading (e.g., "Edit / Solstice · 01", "Pieces / 24 total", "Release / Winter · 11·26").
- **View all label:** The label on the view-all CTA button.
- **View all URL:** The link for the view-all button. Falls back to the collection URL if left blank.

#### Source & Display

- **Full width:** When enabled, the section stretches edge-to-edge.
- **Collection:** Select the Shopify collection to pull products from.
- **Products to show:** Number of products to display in the carousel (range: 4–16).

#### Style

- **Color scheme:** Choose a color scheme for the section.

#### Spacing

- **Padding top / Padding bottom:** Control vertical spacing around the section.
