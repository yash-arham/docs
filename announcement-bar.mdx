The **Announcement Bar** in the Sentiment theme is an elegant, scrolling marquee designed to highlight your most important messages right at the top of your store. Each announcement is its own block — you can add, remove, and reorder them individually. Perfect for showcasing shipping policies, new collection launches, craftsmanship details, or any other store-wide messages.

### How to Open the Announcement Bar

- From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes**.
- Click **Customize** on the Sentiment theme.
- In the Theme Editor, expand **Header** in the left sidebar and select **Announcement bar**.

---

### Section Settings

Click the **Announcement bar** section (not an individual block) to access these settings:

- **Show free shipping notice:** Toggle on to display a dedicated free shipping message alongside your announcements.
- **Shipping message:** The text shown when the free shipping notice is enabled (e.g., "Free shipping on orders over \$500").
- **Concierge link URL:** Paste the page link you want customers directed to when they click the concierge label (e.g., a contact or personal shopping page).
- **Concierge link text:** The label shown for the concierge link (e.g., "Concierge").
- **Color scheme:** Choose from the available preset color combinations. Click **Edit scheme** to customize colors further.
- **Marquee speed:** Controls how fast the text scrolls. The default is **38**. A lower number means a faster animation speed.
- **Marquee direction:** Set the scroll direction — **Right to left** (default) or Left to right.
- **Padding:** Set the top and bottom spacing inside the announcement bar (default is **10 px** each).

### Managing Announcement Blocks

Each scrolling message is an individual block. You can add, edit, and remove blocks independently.

**To add an announcement:**

- In the left sidebar under **Announcement bar**, click **\+ Add Announcement**.
- A new block will appear. Click it to open its settings on the right.
- Enter your message in the **Announcement text** field.

**To edit an announcement:**

- Click any existing announcement block in the left sidebar (e.g., _Announcement – Complimentary s..._).
- Update the **Announcement text** on the right panel.

**To remove an announcement:**

- Click the block you want to delete.
- Click **Remove block** at the bottom of the right panel.

---

### Save Your Changes

Click **Save** in the top-right corner of the Theme Editor to apply all changes.
