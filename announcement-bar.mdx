title: "Announcement bar"

The **Announcement Bar** in the Sentiment theme is an elegant, scrolling marquee designed to highlight your most important messages right at the top of your store. Perfect for showcasing shipping policies, new collection launches, craftsmanship details, or any other store-wide announcements. You can easily customize the text, color scheme, and shipping message using the theme editor — giving customers key information before they even start browsing.

### How to Configure the Announcement Bar

- From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes**.
- Click **Customize** on the Sentiment theme.
- In the Theme Editor, select the **Announcement bar** section from the left sidebar (under Header).
- Adjust the settings and color scheme according to your preferences.
- Click **Save** to apply your changes.

### How to Configure the Announcement Bar Settings

- **Marquee text:** Enter the scrolling message(s) you want displayed in the bar. Separate multiple messages with a bullet or dot (e.g., "Complimentary shipping on orders over \$500 · New collection available now · Each piece hand-finished by our atelier · GIA-certified stones") so they rotate continuously across the bar.
- **Show free shipping notice:** Toggle this on to display a dedicated free shipping message alongside your marquee text.
- **Shipping message:** Enter the specific shipping text you want shown when the free shipping notice is enabled (e.g., "Free shipping on orders over \$500").
- **Concierge link URL:** Paste the link or page you want customers directed to when they click the concierge link (e.g., a contact or personal shopping page).
- **Concierge link text:** Customize the label shown for the concierge link (e.g., "Concierge").
- **Color scheme:** Choose from the available preset color combinations to match the announcement bar with your store's branding. Click **Edit scheme** to further customize colors if needed.
