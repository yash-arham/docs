---
title: "Cart drawer"
---

The **Cart Drawer** is a slide-out panel that opens when a customer adds a product to their cart or clicks the bag icon in the header. It displays all cart line items, a free shipping progress bar, gift wrapping, an order note, and the checkout button — all without leaving the current page.

The cart drawer is a built-in layout-level component, not a standalone section. It is configured via **Theme Settings → Cart**.

### How to Configure the Cart Drawer

- From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes** and click **Customize**.
- Click the **Theme settings** icon (gear icon) in the left sidebar.
- Select **Cart** from the settings list.
- Set the **Cart style** to **Slide-out drawer**.
- Configure the remaining settings and click **Save**.

### Settings

For full details on all cart configuration options — including free shipping threshold, gift wrapping, and order notes — see the [Cart section in Theme Settings](theme-settings).
