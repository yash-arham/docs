title: "Installation Guide"
---

Follow the steps below to purchase, install, and activate the **Sentiment Shopify Theme**.

### 1. Sentiment Theme Installation

Follow the steps below to install the theme on your Online Store

1. In your Shopify admin, navigate to **Online Store \> Themes**.
2. Scroll down to the Discover Themes section.
3. Click on **Visit Theme Store**.
4. In the Shopify Theme Store, search for Sentiment theme.
5. Click **Try Theme** to add it to your store.
6. The theme will be added to your store.
7. Then click on **buy** to purchase the theme.

### 2. Try Sentiment for Free

Just like other premium themes on the Shopify Theme Store, **Sentiment includes a free trial**. You can explore, customize, and test all features before purchasing. When you're ready to go live, simply purchase the theme to keep it active on your store.

How to Try Sentiment:

1. Visit the **Shopify Theme Store** and **select Sentiment**.
2. Click **Try theme** to add it to your Theme Library.
3. In your Shopify admin, open **Online Store \> Themes**.
4. Under **Theme Library**, click **Customize** to start editing your store, or use **Preview** to see how Sentiment looks before customizing.
