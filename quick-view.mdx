---
title: "Quick view"
---

The **Quick View** is a JavaScript-driven modal that lets customers preview a product and add it to their cart without navigating away from the current page. It opens when a customer clicks the quick view trigger on a product card in grids, carousels, or collection pages.

Quick view is a built-in theme component — it is not a standalone section and does not have its own settings panel in the theme editor. It renders automatically wherever product cards appear throughout the store.
